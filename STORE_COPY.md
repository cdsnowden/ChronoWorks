## FAQ (for support page or app store listing)

**Q: How much does ChronoWorks cost?**
A: ChronoWorks is currently free to download and use. We may introduce optional premium features in the future.

**Q: How many employees can I add?**
A: Unlimited! There are no per-employee fees.

**Q: Does ChronoWorks work offline?**
A: Employees can clock in/out offline, and data will sync when they reconnect to the internet.

**Q: Can I export data to my payroll service?**
A: Yes! Export payroll data to CSV format, which works with most payroll services including QuickBooks, ADP, Paychex, and Gusto.

**Q: How does GPS verification work?**
A: When an employee clocks in, ChronoWorks captures their GPS location. Managers can see where each clock-in occurred on a map.

**Q: Can employees clock in from home?**
A: Yes, employees can clock in from any location. GPS tracking shows where they clocked in, which you can review.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes! All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use Firebase (Google Cloud) infrastructure with enterprise-grade security.

**Q: Can I manage multiple businesses?**
A: Yes! Account managers can oversee multiple companies from one account.

**Q: What if an employee forgets to clock out?**
A: Managers receive notifications for missed clock-outs and can edit time entries as needed.

**Q: Can employees swap shifts?**
A: This feature is coming in a future update! For now, managers can reassign shifts manually.
