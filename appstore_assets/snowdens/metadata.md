### Description
Snowden's Time Clock is the official employee time tracking app for Snowden's Jewelers.

CLOCK IN/OUT
- Quick one-tap clock in and out
- GPS location verification
- Optional photo capture
- Automatic overtime calculations

VIEW YOUR SCHEDULE
- See your shifts at a glance
- Get notified of schedule changes
- Check who else is working

TRACK YOUR HOURS
- View your time entries for the current pay period
- See regular and overtime hours
- Review your complete work history

REQUEST TIME OFF
- Submit PTO requests directly in the app
- Track your time-off balance
- Get notified when requests are approved

FOR MANAGERS
- Real-time dashboard showing who's working
- Approve or deny time-off requests
- Edit time entries as needed
- Export payroll reports

This app is for authorized Snowden's Jewelers employees only. Contact your manager for login credentials.
